Short Bio

Lei Zhang is an Assistant Professor in the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Shenzhen University, Shenzhen, Guangdong, China. He received the BEng degree from the Advanced Class of Electronics and Information Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, Hubei, China, and the MSc and PhD degrees from Simon Fraser University, Burnaby, BC, Canada (Supervisor: Prof. Jiangchuan Liu). His current research interests include multimedia systems and applications, mobile computing, edge computing, social networking, and Internet of Things.
